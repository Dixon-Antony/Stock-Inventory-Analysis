Retail Store Stock Inventory Analytics

Inventory Analytics

This dataset contains a lot of historical sales data of a Brazilian top retailer. Basic Questions of every retailer: How much inventory should I carry? Too much inventory means working capital costs, operational costs and a complex operation whereas lack of inventory leads to lost sales, unhappy customers and a damaged brand. This is why short-term forecasting is so important in the retail and consumer goods industry.
